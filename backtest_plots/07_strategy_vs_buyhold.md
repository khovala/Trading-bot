## Analysis

### Why Strategy Outperforms
1. **Market Timing**: By avoiding trades when market momentum is strongly negative (< -3%), we skip periods of systematic decline
2. **Mean Reversion**: Captures reversions to the mean, generating profits in ranging markets
3. **Active Management**: 103 trades allow us to lock in gains and cut losses

### Tickers Where Buy & Hold Wins
1. **TATN, LKOH, POLY**: These tickers may have had strong uptrends during the period that the mean reversion strategy misinterpreted as overextended

### Risk-Adjusted Comparison
- Strategy shows more consistent returns across different market conditions
- Buy & Hold had higher variance with some big winners but also big losers

## Conclusion
The strategy significantly outperforms Buy & Hold by:
- Generating +121K vs -16K PnL
- Reducing exposure to severe drawdowns
- Converting losing positions (POLY, LKOH) losses into manageable amounts through active exits
